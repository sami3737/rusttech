About

RustTech is a server built on solid foundations and hours of Rust experience. RustTech has a range of plugins including Homes, TP's, and Kits, as well as high gather rates to encourage a more action rather than grinding hours on end. Also plugins such as AutoAuthorization to enhance your experience by making it quicker to add friends to toolcupboards and SuperLoot increases the chances of finding higher tier items to decrease the grinding aspect of rust.
